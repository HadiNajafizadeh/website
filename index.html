About Me

I am an Electrical Engineer with a deep passion for Power Systems and their role in shaping the future of energy. Throughout my academic and professional journey, I've focused on integrating advanced techniques like AI to improve power system reliability and resiliency. I'm always eager to learn and explore innovative solutions, especially in areas like smart grid management and renewable energy integration.

Projects

Developing a Dynamic Bayesian Network framework to assess and enhance the resiliency of power systems facing cascading failures, integrating probabilistic methods and AI techniques for predictive analysis and risk mitigation.

Conducted comprehensive analysis to identify and rank weak components in power systems by employing Maximum a Posteriori (MAP) inference within Bayesian Networks, contributing to improved system reliability and maintenance strategies.

Simulated and analyzed the performance of Distributed Energy Resource-based microgrids using droop control, focusing on stability, load sharing, and integration with renewable energy sources.

Developed and optimized an AC-constrained transmission expansion plan using GAMS, focusing on minimizing costs and enhancing network reliability under multiple scenarios.
